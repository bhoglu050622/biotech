## Technology & Approach

### Brain-Computer Interface (BCI)
- **Definition**: Revolutionary technology that allows direct communication between the brain and external devices
- **Function**: Captures brain signals and translates them into commands
- **Applications**: 
  - Control digital systems
  - Provide real-time feedback on brain activity
  - Monitor cognitive states (focus, relaxation, stress)
  - Transform healthcare, wellness, and neurotherapy

### Equilibrium Psychology (EqP)
- **Foundation**: Developed from Equilibrium Thinking
- **Approach**: Novel psychological framework balancing negative and positive aspects of human experience
- **Methodology**: 
  - Binary approach (0's and 1's) like modern machines
  - Dual-process approach with two key concepts:
    - "Be at it"
    - "Beat it"
  - Integrates positive and negative psychology
  - Fosters mental resilience and growth
